Ravioli

Description

Ravioli is the most famous and traditional stuffed pasta. They are those that have a square shape and are usually filled with meat. As a curiosity, ravioli were already served in a papal conclave in the 16th century and were filled with bacon: bocato di cardinale!

The most common thing is to go eat them at a restaurant or buy them already filled, but what about trying to make them yourself? This particular recipe is to prepare a ravioli dish with ricotta, parmesan and ham.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Ricotta
  • Parmesan
  • Ham
  • Arugula

Steps

  1. For the dough we mix 100 grams of wheat flour and 1 egg. We knead everything until we have a smooth and spongy dough.
  2. Then, with the help of a rolling pin, we stretch the dough and give it the shape of a ravioli.
  3. For the filling, we cut the cheese, ham and arugula well, we stir to try to keep the quantities of each tortellini equal and with the help of a teaspoon we put a little of the mixture into each pasta. Then we seal with another piece as if it were a dumpling.
  4. Finally, cook it for a few minutes in a boiling pot. Normally when the pasta stays afloat it will be ready.
