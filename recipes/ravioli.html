Ravioli

Description

Ravioli is the most famous and traditional stuffed pasta. They are those that have a square shape and are usually filled with meat. As a curiosity, ravioli were already served in a papal conclave in the 16th century and were filled with bacon: bocato di cardinale!

The most common thing is to go eat them at a restaurant or buy them already filled, but what about trying to make them yourself? This particular recipe is to prepare a ravioli dish with ricotta, parmesan and ham.
